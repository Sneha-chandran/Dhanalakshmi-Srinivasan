DHANALAKSHMI SRINIVASAN COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING

coimbatore

Nestled away from the odds of the city life, located in aesthetically designed buildings along the Western Ghats in 19 acres of sylvan surrounding, is DSCE. The college over the years continues to leave behind its indelible footprints on young lives through transformative education, nurturing innovation and extensive collaboration.DSCE, in alignment with its vision “Towards Excellence “, is accredited by NAAC with A+ (3.42/4) in its very first cycle of accreditation. All the eligible courses( CIvil, CSE, ECE, EEE, Mech and MBA) are provisionally accredited by NBA. CSE, ECE, EEE, Mech and MBA courses are permanently affiliated to Anna University, Chennai. The college is categorised under 2(f) and 12(B) of the UGC Act.The college is meticulously administrated by Shri. P. Neelaraj, Secretary, a highly self disciplined visionary with more than three decades of experience in the field of education. His expertise has paved way for DSCE to achieve a consistent, steady and quality growth in the filed of technical education, over the years. He is supported by his son, Dr. N. Vinoth, Director, who with his futuristic vision, thinks innovatively propelling the college to move forward in a competitive world
